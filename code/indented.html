ABOUT

AN ORGANIZATION SINCE 1980?

The Tsukuba International Student Association is a non-profit student-run organization working to connect Japanese and international students of the university mainly by creating monthly events. The still young organization appeared in 1980 during the "pioneer days" of Tsukuba when the school had just recently been transferred from Tokyo...

EVENTS

WELCOMING PARTY

Every October and April, we organize and host a welcoming party in Tsukuba to kick off the new semesters. More than 100 students from all backgrounds and ethnicities gather to enjoy the night socializing, and even dancing. Our mission is to connect as many people as possible and provide a welcoming atmosphere in the usually quiet city of Tsukuba...
